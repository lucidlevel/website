Story about me

A Dreamer. A Creator. A Moonshot Maker.

Hello, I am Ethan.

I want to help people who have a desire to succeed at a high level. Who aren't interested in mediocrity. Who aren't satisfied with half baked attempts. I want to help them sieze their dreams.

I am a brand strategist and designer based in Dublin, Ireland. I help businesses find the story behind their brand, the reason why they started the business to begin with. Together we shape and position that into a brand that resonates with their audience.

I focus on Solopreneurs, StartUps and SMEs. Large business can afford to spend 5 or 6 figures for strategy. Most strategists exist in that space. There is very little help for the small fish, I am here to help them. Small can still do great things! Get it right at the start and you can go a long way.

Personally, I love making photomanipulation art, which you can check out on my instagram. And have a deep passion for music. I am a failed "rockstar" in a past life, but that passion never dies.
