ANXEC Technologies

ANXEC is a new technology start-up focusing on wearable augmented reality, AR. They want to create devices that fully intergrate into life, instead of sucking us into the endless voids of the internet. The market, though highly excited for, are very cautious about this AR space; due to the failures of Google glass and the underwhelming respones to the other players in the market.
As a brand, they are walking on eggshells. One wrong step and their reputation crumbles. Something a startup can not recover from. So as a brand it is very important that they don't over hype themselves, but rather focus on their reason why and build their business one small piece at a time. Under promising and over delivering every time. Slowly forming solid foundations to become a major player in this space.

ANXEC believe technology shouldn't get in the way of life, but enhance it. Tech should intergrate into our lives, freeing us from our zombie states, glued to our devices. Don't let life pass you by. Be present.

uncAged apparel

uncAged are a street wear/skateboarder fashion brand with a punk rock attitude. They want to rage agaisnt the machine. They crave the anarchy. Brands like this are always great fun to work with, they know who they are and speak to a really specific group of people who believe the same as them.
The challenging part for their brand is to create a coherent disorder. This can be tricky when they have a belief that anything goes, as the brand can be pulled apart in many different directions. We have to find their core truth behind them, and always tie everything back to that. A thread of structure among the chaos.

uncAged are for the misfits, for the lovers of disorder. Verging on the line between randomness and choas. We do not conform to your rules of life and society. You can not control us. We are uncAged.

Tailored Chat AI

Tailored Chat AI create custom AI chat bots. Each built specifically for each business. Their AI chat bots improve over time with each conversation with their customers, and can solve 95% of the issues faced.
As a brand Tailored Chat AI is targeting the corporate business sector. So focus for them must be on B2B (business to business) relations. Business love their numbers and figures and prioritise time/money saving services. But behind all of that it is still individual people who make the decisions. Finding the balance between the business logic and emotional trust is the key factor for Tailored Chats brand.

At Tailored Chat we know that 80% of the headaches are caused by 20% of the customers. We believe that an AI can solve 95% of these annoying problems. Smoothing out the difficult customers, saving you time, money and stress.
